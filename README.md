# Property AI Toolkit

Two AI tools for property professionals.

| Tool | What it does |
|---|---|
| **RAG** | Your AI that answers questions about your listings and documents |
| **HAT** | Your AI that analyses market conditions and finds historical patterns |

---

## RAG — Your Property Knowledge Base

Your client asks: *"Does this condo allow pets?"* or *"What's the maintenance fee?"*

Instead of digging through PDFs, you ask your AI. It reads all your brochures, listings, and contracts — and answers instantly.

**What you need to give it:**
- Your property brochures (PDF)
- Listing fact sheets
- Standard contracts
- Any FAQs you've written

---

## HAT — Your Market Timing Analyst

You ask: *"Is now a good time to buy in District 15?"* or *"How does this market compare to 2018?"*

The HAT loads 20+ years of property market data, finds the historical periods that looked most like today, and tells you what happened to prices next.

---

## Getting Started

Send this repo to your developer or AI agent. The file `AGENT.md` has everything they need to build both tools.

**You don't need to understand the code.** You just need to:
1. Collect your documents (for RAG)
2. Get your historical market data (for HAT) — CSV export from your CRM, or URA/HDB data
3. Hand it to your developer

---

## What you'll end up with

- A chat interface where you or your clients can ask questions about properties
- A market analysis tool that shows historical analogs and what happened next
- Both running on your machine, privately, at near-zero cost
