## Singapore Property Cycle — Key Historical Episodes

| Period | What happened | Lesson |
|---|---|---|
| 1996–1998 | Asian Financial Crisis. Prices fell 40–45% peak to trough. Took 10 years to recover. | External shocks + overleveraged buyers = long recovery |
| 2003 | SARS. Sharp but short. V-shaped recovery within 18 months. | Health shocks are temporary if fundamentals are sound |
| 2007–2009 | GFC. Prices fell ~25%. Recovered faster than 1998 due to government stimulus. | Government intervention shortens cycles |
| 2011–2013 | Post-GFC boom. ABSD introduced Dec 2011 (SC 7%, foreigner 15%). Volume fell, prices held. | ABSD slows volume before prices |
| 2018 | ABSD hike (SC 12%→15%, foreigner 15%→20%). Volume -30%, prices flat for 18 months. | Hikes cool sentiment immediately, prices lag |
| 2020 | COVID. Prices barely dipped — government support + low rates + limited supply. | Low rates + supply constraint = resilient prices |
| 2022–2023 | ABSD hike (foreigner to 60%). Foreign demand collapsed. Local demand absorbed. | Foreigners are price-sensitive. Locals are not. |

## Cooling Measure Cycle (Singapore-specific)

Government pattern — reliable over 30 years:
1. Prices rise >8% YoY for 2+ quarters → ABSD hike or LTV tightening
2. Volume falls 20–30% within 2 quarters of measure
3. Prices lag volume — typically flat or -5% over 12–18 months
4. Government holds measures until volume recovers
5. When volume recovers + prices stabilise → gradual relaxation

**Current status (Q1 2026):** ABSD at 60% for foreigners, 20% for SC second property. No new measures since 2023. Prices slowing on their own — government unlikely to hike again unless prices re-accelerate above 5% YoY.

## HDB Upgrader Cycle

BTO completions → MOP at 5 years → upgrader wave hits private OCR market.
- 2019–2021 BTO launches completing 2024–2026 → MOP 2029–2031
- BUT many upgraders start looking 1–2 years before MOP
- This is the structural demand driver for OCR private in 2026–2028

## Key Terminology

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| CCR | Core Central Region (Districts 9, 10, 11, Downtown Core, Sentosa) |
| RCR | Rest of Central Region |
| OCR | Outside Central Region (heartlands — most upgrader activity) |
| PSF | Price per square foot |
| PPI | Private Property Price Index (URA quarterly) |
| ABSD | Additional Buyer's Stamp Duty |
| TDSR | Total Debt Servicing Ratio (55% cap) |
| LTV | Loan-to-Value ratio |
| MOP | Minimum Occupation Period (HDB — 5 years before resale/upgrade) |
| GLS | Government Land Sales (supply pipeline) |
| SORA | Singapore Overnight Rate Average (benchmark for home loans since 2023) |
| CEA | Council for Estate Agencies (property agent licensing body) |
| TOP | Temporary Occupation Permit (completion of new development) |
