# Hey Cuz 👋

I built you something. It's called **Shifu** — your personal AI property advisor.

Shifu does two things:
- Tells you the selling points, comparables, and rental yield for any listing
- Tells you what the Singapore market has historically done in conditions like today

You don't need to understand how it works. Just follow the steps below.

---

## How to install Shifu

**Step 1 — Send this link to your AI agent (Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, whatever you use):**

> https://github.com/whooshinglander/property-shifu

**Step 2 — Tell your AI agent this:**

> *"Go to this GitHub link. Read the file called BUILD.md. Build Shifu for me exactly as described. Tell me when it's ready and show me the test outputs."*

That's it. Your AI agent handles everything else — downloading the data, setting up the system, testing it. You just wait for it to say it's done.

**Step 3 — Once your AI agent says it's ready, give it your listing PDFs:**

> *"Here are my listing brochures. Load them into Shifu."*

Drop your PDF files and your AI agent will feed them to Shifu.

## How to talk to Shifu

Copy any of these prompts and send them to Shifu. Change the parts in [brackets].

---

### 📋 Factsheet — before a client meeting

> *"Shifu, create a factsheet for [development name], [bedroom type], [sqft] sqft, asking $[price], unit [floor-stack], [facing direction]."*

**Example:**
> *"Shifu, create a factsheet for Parc Esta, 3-bedroom + study, 1,001 sqft, asking $2,400,000, unit #08-12, north-facing."*

Shifu gives you: selling points, PSF vs district average, last 3 comparable transactions, rental yield, ideal buyer profile, and one line you can say to your client.

---

### 💬 Selling points — when a client asks "why this unit?"

> *"Shifu, what are the top selling points for [development name], [unit type]?"*

**Example:**
> *"Shifu, what are the top selling points for Riversails, 2-bedroom, 979 sqft, $1,400,000?"*

---

### 📊 Market timing — when a client asks "should I buy now or wait?"

> *"Shifu, my client wants to buy a [type] in [district or area]. Budget $[amount]. Is now a good time?"*

**Example:**
> *"Shifu, my client wants to buy a resale 3-bedroom in District 19. Budget $1.8M. Is now a good time?"*

---

### 🏘️ District analysis — when a client asks about a specific area

> *"Shifu, what is happening in [district] right now? Is it a good area to buy?"*

**Example:**
> *"Shifu, what is happening in District 15 right now? Is it a good area to buy?"*

---

### 💰 Rental yield — when a client asks about investment return

> *"Shifu, what is the estimated rental yield for [development name], [bedroom type], [sqft] sqft, $[price]?"*

**Example:**
> *"Shifu, what is the estimated rental yield for The M, 2-bedroom, 753 sqft, $1,999,999?"*

---

### 🔍 Comparables — when you need recent transaction data

> *"Shifu, show me the last [number] transactions for [development name]."*

**Example:**
> *"Shifu, show me the last 5 transactions for Ecopolitan in District 19."*

---

### 📈 Price trend — when a client asks if prices are going up or down

> *"Shifu, what is the price trend for [district or area] in the last 12 months?"*

**Example:**
> *"Shifu, what is the price trend for OCR resale condos in the last 12 months?"*

---

### 🆚 Compare two properties — when a client is deciding between two units

> *"Shifu, compare [property 1] and [property 2]. Which is better value?"*

**Example:**
> *"Shifu, compare Grandeur 8 3-bedroom at $2,088,000 in D20 and Braddell View 3-bedroom at $1,750,000 in D20. Which is better value?"*

---

### 📝 WhatsApp message — when you want to send something to a client fast

> *"Shifu, write me a short WhatsApp message to send to my client about [property]. Keep it under 5 lines."*

**Example:**
> *"Shifu, write me a short WhatsApp message to send to my client about Dahlia Park 4-bedroom, $2,190,000, D17, freehold. Keep it under 5 lines."*

---

### 🤔 Client objection — when a client says it's too expensive or bad timing

> *"Shifu, my client thinks [property] is overpriced at $[price]. What does the data say?"*

**Example:**
> *"Shifu, my client thinks $1,400,000 for a 2-bedder at Riversails is overpriced. What does the data say?"*

---

## Things Shifu cannot do (yet)

- It does not know your own past deals unless you tell it
- It cannot pull real-time new launch prices (developer prices not public until after sale)
- It does not give legal or financial advice — that's your job
- If it says "data pending", tell your AI agent to run the data update first

---

## One last thing

Shifu gets smarter the more you use it. After every good conversation, your AI agent updates Shifu's memory file (`SHIFU.md`). Over time it remembers your market, your typical clients, and the patterns you've noticed in your own deals.
